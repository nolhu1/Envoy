# Launch Legal Readiness Checklist

This is an operational checklist, not legal advice.

- Privacy policy drafted and published.
- Terms of service drafted and published.
- Support contact visible to customers.
- Data deletion request process documented.
- Workspace deletion/export process documented.
- Gmail API compliance reviewed.
- OpenAI usage disclosure reviewed.
- Security contact and incident response owner assigned.
- Provider token storage and revocation process reviewed.
- Customer data retention policy reviewed.
